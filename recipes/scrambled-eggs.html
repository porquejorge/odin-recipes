Duckling's Quacking Scrambled Eggs

Duckling's Ingredients

  • eggs
  • butter
  • salt
  • chilli flakes
    Even little ducks can follow these steps
  1. Melt butter over low to medium heat
  2. Mix eggs in a cup, then pour them on the melted butter
  3. Immediately start stirring
  4. Keep stirring until you reach desired consistency. We'll keep them more liquidy because ducks like to swim
  5. Top with salt and chilli flakes
