Mama Duck's Favorite Fruit Dumplings

Mama Duck's Ingredients

  • cream cheese
  • eggs
  • flour
  • oil
  • salt
  • fruit

Steps to take on your journey

  1. Work the cream cheese, egg, flour, salt and oil into a dough.
  2. Cut the dough in half and roll out each part into a sheet. Cut the dough into squares, wrap the fruit in each, close tightly and round into a dumpling shape.
  3. Place the prepared dumplings in boiling water, separate them from the bottom after a while and cook them for about 3-5 minutes as soon as they appear on the surface.
  4. Pour melted butter over the dumplings on the plate, sprinkle with sugar and add a dollop of sour cream.
  5. Sprinkle bugs on top if your heart desires
